Aqua-Tech Tip #1

When cleaning a component or your entire computer, you should turn it off before cleaning.

Never spray or squirt any liquid onto any computer component.

This can lead to hardware damage.

Aqua-Tech Tip #2

Never ever sit your laptop on a couch cushion, or on a mattress, or anything soft.

This hinders airflow through the little slot on the bottom and sides of your machine causing your fans and laptop to work harder.

Aqua-Tech Tip #3

Accidentally closed a tab?

Simply press Ctrl + Shift + T to reopen the most recently closed tab and get back to what you were doing.

(Cmd + Shift + T on Macs).

Aqua-Tech Tip #2

Pressing Windows button + ‘L’ will lock your computer immediately and you will need password to access it again (if the password is set).

This is very useful to quickly lock your PC when you leaving the desk for some time.
